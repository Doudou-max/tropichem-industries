Quality Control System





Raw Materials Control

We are the only industrial chain control factory in China. The main material is produced by ourselves to guarantee the finished product quality.

We store the raw materials in a proper warehouse with proper humidity and temperature strictly to ensure its quality in order to guarantee the finished product quality.







Production Control

We have 8 modern workshops in factory to produce best quality product as well as meet customers’ capacity requirement. Full covering monitor of production to guarantee the production and product quality to avoid any risks.





R &D Control

We have professional R &D staffs to improve the products more efficient when put into the finished product. They at least get Bachelor's Degree.

For each batch, we will store backups of samples for two years in case of a quality issue.






Environmentally Friendly Control

We think highly of environment-friendly treatment. We know deeply environment sustainable development have a big influence on descendant and future. We not only focus on business but also responsible for all aspects. 






Packing And Warehouse Control

We store the finished products in the proper warehouse with proper humidity and temperature strictly to ensure their quality. All packing materials are bought from AAA credit packing factories in China, which is strong enough to protect the product against destroy and pollution






Sales Ability

Our sales have averaged more than 5 years of experience in exporting, and are proficient in all processes of business operation which can efficiently fulfill customer needs. We currently export to more than 70 countries, and the partners include Dupont, Unilever, and so on. We can also help you to supply registration documents of the importing in different countries.
